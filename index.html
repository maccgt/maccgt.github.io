    Minorities at the College of Computing

    M@CC supports minority students in the college of computing as they pursue their academic and professional goals. We provide space for students to openly discuss questions, experiences, and opinions on computer science and the tech industry. We also serve as a safe platform for students to connect with prestigious companies and organizations in the tech space.
